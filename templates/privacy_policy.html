Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Workout Timer ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our workout timer application and its WHOOP integration features.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Workout Data: Workout descriptions, intervals, durations, and performance metrics you create or input
  • Session Data: Information about your workout sessions, including completion rates and performance ratings
  • Preferences: Your workout preferences, goals, and settings

2.2 WHOOP Integration Data

When you connect your WHOOP account, we may access:

  • Recovery scores and metrics
  • Sleep performance data
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Workout history and strain scores
  • Basic profile information (email, user ID)

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and performance
  • Technical Data: Device information, browser type, and app version

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • Generate and store your workout plans
  • Track your workout sessions and progress
  • Provide workout recommendations and insights
  • Enable AI-powered workout generation

3.2 WHOOP Integration Purposes

  • Provide personalized workout recommendations based on your recovery data
  • Analyze performance patterns in relation to recovery and sleep
  • Generate insights about your training optimization
  • Create comprehensive workout summaries

3.3 Analytics and Improvement

  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Develop new features and capabilities
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize performance

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 WHOOP Data Sharing

We do not share your WHOOP data with third parties. Your WHOOP data is used solely for:

  • Personalized workout recommendations within our app
  • Performance analysis and insights
  • Integration with your workout sessions

4.2 Service Providers

  • OpenAI: For AI-powered workout generation (no personal data shared)
  • Hosting Services: For app deployment and data storage

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • WHOOP access tokens are securely stored
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • View and download your workout data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Disconnect your WHOOP account at any time
  • Opt out of data collection (may limit app functionality)

6.2 WHOOP Integration Control

  • Connect or disconnect your WHOOP account anytime
  • Control which WHOOP data is accessed
  • Revoke app permissions through WHOOP settings

7. Data Retention

  • Workout Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • WHOOP Data: Cached temporarily for app functionality
  • Session Data: Retained for analysis and insights
  • Account Deletion: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@workout-timer.app
  • GitHub: https://github.com/Rarabu1/workout-timer

For WHOOP-specific privacy concerns: Please also review WHOOP's Privacy Policy at https://www.whoop.com/privacy

12. WHOOP OAuth Specific Information

OAuth Scope and Permissions:

  • read:recovery - Access to recovery scores and metrics
  • read:workouts - Access to workout history and performance data
  • read:profile - Access to basic profile information
  • read:cycles - Access to training cycles and patterns
  • read:sleep - Access to sleep performance data

Note: We only request read access to your WHOOP data. We cannot modify or write data to your WHOOP account.
